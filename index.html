Features

Everything you need
to run your service business

From the first customer call to the final signed report — KitchenDesk covers the entire service workflow.

🏢

Customer & Site Management

Organize all your customers and their locations. Link equipment and service history to each account. Search and filter in seconds.

⚙️

Equipment Registry

Track every piece of equipment with model, serial number, photos, and operational status. Know the history of every asset at a glance.

🔧

Service Tickets

Create, assign, and resolve service tickets from your phone. Track time, parts used, and technician notes in one place.

📄

AI-Powered Reports

Generate professional service reports with the help of AI. Export to PDF and share with customers directly from the app.

📦

Inventory Control

Track spare parts and stock levels. Inventory automatically decrements when parts are used on a service report.

🔍

Equipment Lookup

Search equipment specs automatically using AI-powered lookup. Identify parts and manuals without leaving the app.
